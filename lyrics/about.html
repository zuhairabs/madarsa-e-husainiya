About App
In the service of Imam-e-Zamana (a.t.f.s) and Janabe Zehra (s.a), this is a step taken towards azadari to make it easier for the Reciters to have any Lyrics easily in hand.
Nauha Lyrics is a collection of Lyrics of the Nauhas recited by the Nauhakhwan every year plus Manaqabat and Marsiyas.
We will be consistently updating our database with new lyrics to help users getting almost every lyrics.

Special thanks to all those khadime Aale Rasool who helped with their efforts to launch this App.
Please Recite Salwat for Hadiya of All Marhoom Momeneen and Momenaat!
